About Lister

History

Lister Technologies was established in 2000, headquartered in Chennai. In 2004, we began working as an extended professional services team of a leading e-mail marketing company and started our journey with digital marketing services. Three years later, we began working with a sizeable ed-tech customer and focused on retail and e-commerce, simultaneously building our Salesforce Practice by becoming one of the first partners for Salesforce in India.

Leadership

Srinivasagopalan Chary founded Lister Technologies in 2001 and serves as its CEO, steering the company’s strategy, vision, corporate governance and P&L. In his career, he has also served as a founding member of Sify Technologies Limited and worked in the supply chain function of the Abu Dhabi National Oil Company. He holds an undergraduate degree in mechanical engineering and a postgraduate diploma in management from the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad.

Vasan Sampath heads Lister’s POD-CRM function, driving POD’s P&L, engaging with employees and driving business maintenance, growth and acquisition. He has been part of Lister Technologies since 2006. Before this, he served as the founding director of Quoram Consulting for nine years and as a product manager in Tube Investments of India Limited for eight years.

Culture and Values

People First

Our people are an intrinsic part of our business, and we continuously cultivate a spirit of belonging, caring, and collaboration among them. Deep affection for each other, mutual respect, and synergy are core to our collective identity: without every person's contribution, we wouldn't be where we are today.

Integrity and Ethics

Fairmindedness, trust, scrupulousness, openness, and transparency are second nature to us. Whether it is paying our people or our partners on time or quoting fair prices to our clients, we ensure that we are ethical in our engagements with employees, vendors, clients, and public institutions.

Life at Lister

It’s not just about the work. Work gets done, usually, without much supervision. However, your actual development will not happen unless you push yourself, get out of your comfort zone.

Lister gives you a forum to experiment and challenge yourself: in life, after all, you are your best competition.

When you are part of Lister, you reshape your life and career. So,

  • Make friends
  • Create an extended family
  • Share your experience and learnings
  • Listen to others

Women reboot program

At Lister, we recognize that rewarding careers do not always follow a conventional path. We value the diversity, fresh perspective, and wealth of experience that returning professionals can bring. This program aims to empower women to explore new opportunities with Lister. We know you are committed to making a comeback, regardless of the reason or the length of your break.
